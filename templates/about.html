Our story

A small Singapore label.

MISS CLOVER started with one question: what would the everyday tote look like if it were quietly considered?

We're a small studio in Singapore, working with a single silhouette — a structured 8″×11″ leather tote with a suede-lined interior. Two designs to begin: Classic in cream leather, and Maroon in deep burgundy. Both finished by hand, with gold hardware and the same thoughtful interior — lipstick holder, key fob, inner zip.

The clover in our mark is for luck and for the small things that make a day. We hope our bags become one of those for you.
