Our story

A small Singapore label.

MISS CLOVER started with one question: what would the everyday bag look like if it were quietly considered?

We're a small studio in Singapore. Two starting silhouettes — a structured 8″×11″ leather tote with a suede-lined interior, and a soft, wave-quilted padded shoulder bag. Each comes in a handful of colourways, all hand-finished, all detailed with gold-tone clover hardware.

The clover in our mark is for luck and for the small things that make a day. We hope our bags become one of those for you.
